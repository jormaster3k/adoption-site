About Us

We live in Rochester NY, a medium-sized city in western New York. Valerie works in Clinical Trials research, while Jory works in IT as a system administrator. We met in October of 2014, got married in May of 2019, and decided to plant our roots in Rochester so we could begin the next chapter of our lives.

In 2017, we expanded our family by adopting a canine companion into our lives. Grim, now 4 years old, is an affectionate dog who loves cuddling up on the couch, chasing squirrels, and searching the house for his favorite toys!

In our spare time we enjoy cooking food with friends, gaming nights, enjoying our various hobbies, and striving to live each day joyfully. We value continiuous growth and strive to approach every aspect of life with curiousity and openness. We have been together for five years and have built our relationship on balance and honesty.
